Terms and Conditions

1. Agreement

By using our car rental services, you agree to comply with and be bound by the following terms and conditions. Please review them carefully.

2. Rental Requirements

To rent a vehicle from Nago Car Rental, you must meet the following requirements:

  • Be at least 21 years of age
  • Present a valid driver's license held for at least 2 years
  • Provide a valid government-issued identification
  • Present a valid credit card in your name for the security deposit
  • Provide proof of insurance or purchase our coverage options

3. Reservations and Payments

All reservations are subject to vehicle availability. A reservation deposit may be required to secure your booking. We accept major credit cards and bank transfers for payments.

Prices quoted include basic insurance but may exclude additional fees such as tourism tax, airport surcharges, or optional equipment.

4. Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 48 hours or more prior to the scheduled pickup time will receive a full refund. Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice will be subject to a cancellation fee equivalent to one day's rental rate.

No-shows will be charged the full amount of the reservation.

5. Rental Period

The rental period begins at the time specified in your reservation and ends when the vehicle is returned to our location. We offer a 29-minute grace period for returns. After this period, additional charges will apply.

Overtime charges are calculated at 1/5th of the daily rate for each hour overdue, with a maximum of one full day's rental rate for every 5 hours of overtime.

6. Vehicle Use

The vehicle may only be driven by authorized drivers listed on the rental agreement. The vehicle must not be used for:

  • Transporting persons or property for hire
  • Pushing or towing any vehicle, trailer, or other object
  • Any illegal purposes or races
  • Driving on unpaved roads or off-road
  • Transporting hazardous materials

7. Insurance and Liability

All rentals include basic third-party liability insurance as required by Mexican law. Additional coverage options are available for purchase:

  • Collision Damage Waiver (CDW)
  • Theft Protection (TP)
  • Personal Accident Insurance (PAI)
  • Supplemental Liability Insurance (SLI)

Renters are responsible for any damage to the vehicle regardless of fault, up to the full value of the vehicle, unless protected by purchased coverage options.

8. Fuel Policy

Vehicles are provided with a full tank of fuel and must be returned with a full tank. If the vehicle is returned without a full tank, we will refuel it at a rate higher than local fuel prices, plus a service fee.

9. Traffic Violations and Tolls

Renters are responsible for all traffic violations, parking tickets, and tolls incurred during the rental period. Administrative fees will be added for processing any violations received by Nago Car Rental.

10. Vehicle Return

Vehicles must be returned to the specified location during business hours. After-hours returns may be arranged in advance and are subject to additional fees.

The vehicle will be inspected upon return. Any new damage will be documented and charged to the renter.

11. Modifications to Terms

Nago Car Rental reserves the right to modify these terms and conditions at any time. Changes will be effective upon posting to our website. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the modified terms.

12. Governing Law

These terms and conditions are governed by the laws of Mexico and the State of Quintana Roo. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Cancún, Quintana Roo.
